生效日期：2026-06-13

1. 支持渠道

所有支持通过邮箱 deanymrq@gmail.com 提供。我们会尽力在数个工作日内回复（非 7×24 即时支持承诺）。

2. 支持范围

  • 安装与运行问题（含免安装 exe 与安装包）；
  • Pro 许可的安装与激活；
  • 功能使用咨询（clean / files / merge、预设、历史等）；
  • 缺陷反馈与改进建议。

3. 提交问题时请附上

  • 软件版本（运行 xlsx-organizer --version）；

更新通过 GitHub 发布页分发。运行 xlsx-organizer update check 或在界面点击"检查更新"可查看是否有新版本；下载后请用 SHA256SUMS.txt 校验文件完整性。

Support & Invoicing

Effective date: 2026-06-13

1. Support channel

All support is provided by email at deanymrq@gmail.com. We aim to reply within a few business days (this is not a 24/7 instant-support guarantee).

2. What support covers

  • installation and run issues (standalone exe and installer);
  • installing and activating a Pro license;
  • how-to questions (clean / files / merge, presets, history, etc.);
  • bug reports and improvement suggestions.

3. When reporting an issue, please include

  • the software version (run xlsx-organizer --version);
  • your operating system and version;
  • steps to reproduce and the expected result;
  • the relevant report or log — without any sensitive data.

4. Fixes & updates

Updates are distributed via the GitHub releases page. Run xlsx-organizer update check or click "Check for updates" in the GUI to see whether a newer version exists; after downloading, verify integrity against SHA256SUMS.txt.

5. Invoicing

Invoices or receipts for a Pro purchase are available on request. After purchase, email the support address with the invoice title/payee and, if required, a tax number and invoice type. We issue it once payment is confirmed.

6. Contact

deanymrq@gmail.com

This document is general guidance and does not constitute legal advice. Response times and invoice types can be adapted to your actual business and tax situation.
